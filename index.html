    What is Hextech Friends ?

    Hextech Friends is a small utility program for League of Legends.
    It enables you to invite a lot of people really, really fast -
    and that without copy & paste a lot of names (Remember that one guy with a Greek letter in his name?), or adding a lot of people to your friendlist.
